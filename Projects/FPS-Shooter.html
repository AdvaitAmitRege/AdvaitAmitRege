FPS Shooter

About

FPS Shooter is a fast-paced first-person shooter game. Battle against AI or friends in various arenas. Built with Unity and C#.

Project Info

  • 👤Role: Game Developer
  • 🕹️Genre: First Person Shooter
  • 🛠️Engine: Unity (C#)
  • ⏱️Time frame: 5 Months

Introduction

FPS Shooter is a competitive shooter that challenges your reflexes and tactics. Play solo or with friends in action-packed matches.

Features & Highlights

  • 🔫Multiple weapons and upgrades
  • 🗺️Variety of maps and arenas
  • 🤖Challenging AI opponents
  • 🎮Multiplayer support

Fast-Paced Action

Jump into intense firefights with responsive controls and fluid movement.

Tactical Gameplay

Use cover, flanking, and teamwork to outsmart your opponents and win matches.

Immersive Arenas

Battle in a variety of detailed maps, each offering unique tactical opportunities.
