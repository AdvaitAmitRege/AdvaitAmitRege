Lab-1995

About

Lab-1995 is a first-person psychological horror game set in a sealed government research facility abandoned after unethical experiments in 1995. You play as the son of a missing scientist, guided by a voice that seems to be your father but is actually Project HELIX, an AI that has absorbed fragments of his mind.
