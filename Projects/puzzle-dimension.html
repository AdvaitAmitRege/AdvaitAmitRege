Puzzle Dimension

About

Puzzle Dimension is a mobile puzzle game featured in the App Store. Solve mind-bending puzzles and progress through challenging levels. Built with Unity and C#.

Project Info

  • 👤Role: Game Designer & Developer
  • 🕹️Genre: Puzzle
  • 🛠️Engine: Unity (C#)
  • ⏱️Time frame: 4 Months

Introduction

Puzzle Dimension is a creative puzzle game that tests your logic and spatial reasoning. Each level introduces new mechanics and challenges, keeping gameplay fresh and engaging.

Features & Highlights

  • 🧩Unique puzzle mechanics
  • 📱Optimized for mobile devices
  • 🌟Featured in the App Store
  • 🎵Immersive sound and visuals

Challenging Levels

Each level is carefully crafted to provide a unique challenge, encouraging players to think outside the box.

Intuitive Controls

Designed for mobile, the controls are simple and responsive, making gameplay accessible to everyone.

Minimalist Visuals

A clean and modern art style keeps the focus on the puzzles while providing a visually pleasing experience.
